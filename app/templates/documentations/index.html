Who Are We?

InfraLinker is a modern data center infrastructure assets management solution designed to make managing and tracking your data center assets easier and more efficient.

We provide a centralized database that helps eliminate common errors and data leaks associated with traditional methods such as spreadsheets and text documents. Our intuitive and secure platform allows users to take control of their infrastructure, making it easier to manage and track all their assets. Our primary goal is to help businesses streamline their operations and gain better visibility into their data center infrastructure.services.
